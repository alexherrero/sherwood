# Welcome to Sherwood

Sherwood is an automated trading engine and management dashboard. This wiki serves as the central hub for project documentation, setup guides, and progress tracking.

## Overview

The goal of Sherwood is to provide a simple platform for developing and executing algorithmic trading strategies that doesn't require a deep understanding of programming.
